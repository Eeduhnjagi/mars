What is Shares trading?

Shares trading or Stocks trading is essentially buying and selling shares, or Stocks, in public organisations with the aim of making profit. When an investor purchases a Stock, they essentially buy their own share in the company whilst providing it with the capital to put into the business.

Why trade Shares?

Owning Stocks in companies can be beneficial for one’s finances in a number of different ways

Helps build one’s savings and earn passive income

Protects money from inflation and taxes

Offers a possibility to take advantage of the growing economy
